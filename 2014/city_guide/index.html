Get around Lviv

From the Airport

Airport is right at the city border. It is easily reachable by car, public transport or taxi. More information is available at airport website.

Public Transit

Lviv has very diverse public transportation network. Although we strive to hold our conference in the city center, you might want to go somewhere. You have several options:

  • Use Google Maps Transit
  • Use Lviv Router App for offline public transport routes

Hosting

Several hotels and hostels have special discounts for DevFest attendees!

  • Post House Hostel - 10%
  • Old Ukrainian Home Hostel - 15%
  • Coffee Home Hostel - 15%
  • Soviet Home Hostel - 15%
  • Art Hostel - 15%

Enjoy your discounted booking!
